About

Led by Prof. DONG, Zhao Yang, the JC STEM Lab of Future Energy System was established in November 2024 with generous support from the Hong Kong Jockey Club Charities Trust. The Lab serves as a dynamic hub for graduate students, postdoctoral researchers, and visiting scholars.

Its research is centered on Power Systems and Smart Grids, with key areas of focus including System Stability & Reliability, System Planning & Operations, Microgrid / Smart Grid Development, Load Modeling, Integration of Wind/PV/BESS & GPS, Electric Vehicles & Virtual Power Plants (VPP), and Energy Market dynamics. The laboratory has delivered research outputs for multiple government departments and enterprises, and is dedicated to making significant contributions to urban energy conservation, cost reduction, and efficiency enhancement.
