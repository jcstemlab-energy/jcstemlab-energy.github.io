Established in 2024

Led by Professor DONG Zhaoyang, the JC STEM Lab of Future Energy System serves as a dynamic hub for cutting-edge energy research.

Established with generous support from the Hong Kong Jockey Club Charities Trust, our laboratory is dedicated to making significant contributions to urban energy conservation, cost reduction, and efficiency enhancement.
